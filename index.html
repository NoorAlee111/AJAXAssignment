E-commerce, short for electronic commerce, revolutionizes traditional retail by conducting business transactions online. It involves buying and selling goods and services over the internet, eliminating geographical constraints and providing a global marketplace. E-commerce platforms enable businesses to showcase products, process secure payments, and manage inventory seamlessly. Consumers benefit from the convenience of browsing and purchasing from anywhere, often with personalized experiences and targeted recommendations. E-commerce encompasses various models, including B2B (business to business), B2C (business to consumer), and C2C (consumer to consumer). With the continuous evolution of technology, e-commerce continues to shape the way commerce is conducted, fostering efficiency, accessibility, and innovation.
